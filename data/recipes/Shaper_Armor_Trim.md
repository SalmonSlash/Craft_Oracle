# Shaper Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Shaper_Armor_Trim

To craft Shaper Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Shaper Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Shaper Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Shaper Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Terracotta | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
