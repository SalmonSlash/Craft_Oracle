# Cherry Hanging Sign
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cherry_Hanging_Sign

To craft Cherry Hanging Sign (makes 6), you need: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Cherry Log x6.
Ingredients: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Cherry Log x6
