# Lectern
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Lectern

To craft Lectern, you need: Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x4, Bookshelf x1.
Ingredients: Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x4, Bookshelf x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Oak Slab | Oak Slab | Oak Slab
(empty) | Bookshelf | (empty)
(empty) | Oak Slab | (empty)
