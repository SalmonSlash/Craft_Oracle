# Green Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Green_Terracotta

To craft Green Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Green Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Green Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Green Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
