# Coast Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Coast_Armor_Trim

To craft Coast Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Coast Armor Trim x1, Cobblestone x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Coast Armor Trim x1, Cobblestone x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Coast Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Cobblestone | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
