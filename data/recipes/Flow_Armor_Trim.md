# Flow Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Flow_Armor_Trim

To craft Flow Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Flow Armor Trim x1, Breeze Rod x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Flow Armor Trim x1, Breeze Rod x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Flow Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Breeze Rod | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
