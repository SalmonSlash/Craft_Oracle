# Bolt Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Bolt_Armor_Trim

To craft Bolt Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Bolt Armor Trim x1, Block of Copper or Waxed Block of Copper x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Bolt Armor Trim x1, Block of Copper or Waxed Block of Copper x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Bolt Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Block of Copper | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
