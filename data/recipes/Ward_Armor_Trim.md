# Ward Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ward_Armor_Trim

To craft Ward Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Ward Armor Trim x1, Cobbled Deepslate x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Ward Armor Trim x1, Cobbled Deepslate x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Ward Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Cobbled Deepslate | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
