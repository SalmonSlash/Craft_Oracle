# Purple Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Purple_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Purple Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Purple Stained Glass x5, Purple Dye or Purple Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Purple Stained Glass x5, Purple Dye or Purple Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Purple Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
