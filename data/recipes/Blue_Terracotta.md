# Blue Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Blue_Terracotta

To craft Blue Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Blue Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Blue Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Blue Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
