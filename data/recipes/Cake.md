# Cake
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cake

To craft Cake, you need: Milk Bucket x3, Sugar x2, Egg or Blue Egg or Brown Egg x1, Wheat x3.
Ingredients: Milk Bucket x3, Sugar x2, Egg or Blue Egg or Brown Egg x1, Wheat x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Milk Bucket | Milk Bucket | Milk Bucket
Sugar | Egg | Sugar
Wheat | Wheat | Wheat
