# Birch Hanging Sign
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Birch_Hanging_Sign

To craft Birch Hanging Sign (makes 6), you need: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Birch Log x6.
Ingredients: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Birch Log x6
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Iron Chain | (empty) | Iron Chain
Stripped Birch Log | Stripped Birch Log | Stripped Birch Log
Stripped Birch Log | Stripped Birch Log | Stripped Birch Log
