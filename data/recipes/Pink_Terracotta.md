# Pink Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pink_Terracotta

To craft Pink Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Pink Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Pink Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Pink Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
