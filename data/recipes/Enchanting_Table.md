# Enchanting Table
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Enchanting_Table

To craft Enchanting Table, you need: Book x1, Diamond x2, Obsidian x4.
Ingredients: Book x1, Diamond x2, Obsidian x4
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Book | (empty)
Diamond | Obsidian | Diamond
Obsidian | Obsidian | Obsidian
