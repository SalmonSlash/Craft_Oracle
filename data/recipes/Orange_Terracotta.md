# Orange Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Orange_Terracotta

To craft Orange Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Orange Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Orange Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Orange Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
