# White Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/White_Terracotta

To craft White Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, White Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, White Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | White Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
