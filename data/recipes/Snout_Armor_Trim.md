# Snout Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Snout_Armor_Trim

To craft Snout Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Snout Armor Trim x1, Blackstone x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Snout Armor Trim x1, Blackstone x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Snout Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Blackstone | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
