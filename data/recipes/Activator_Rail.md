# Activator Rail
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Activator_Rail

To craft Activator Rail (makes 6), you need: Iron Ingot x6, Stick x2, Redstone Torch x1.
Ingredients: Iron Ingot x6, Stick x2, Redstone Torch x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Iron Ingot | Stick | Iron Ingot
Iron Ingot | Redstone Torch | Iron Ingot
Iron Ingot | Stick | Iron Ingot
