# Gray Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Gray_Terracotta

To craft Gray Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Gray Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Gray Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Gray Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
