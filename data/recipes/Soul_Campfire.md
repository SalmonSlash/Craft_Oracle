# Soul Campfire
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Soul_Campfire

To craft Soul Campfire, you need: Stick x3, Soul Sand or Soul Soil x1, Oak Log or Oak Wood or Birch Log x3.
Ingredients: Stick x3, Soul Sand or Soul Soil x1, Oak Log or Oak Wood or Birch Log x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Stick | (empty)
Stick | Soul Sand | Stick
Oak Log | Oak Log | Oak Log
