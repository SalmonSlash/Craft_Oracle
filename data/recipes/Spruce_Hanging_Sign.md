# Spruce Hanging Sign
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spruce_Hanging_Sign

To craft Spruce Hanging Sign (makes 6), you need: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Spruce Log x6.
Ingredients: Iron Chain x2, Stripped Spruce Log x6
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Iron Chain | (empty) | Iron Chain
Stripped Spruce Log | Stripped Spruce Log | Stripped Spruce Log
Stripped Spruce Log | Stripped Spruce Log | Stripped Spruce Log
