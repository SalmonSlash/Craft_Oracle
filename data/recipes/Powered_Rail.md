# Powered Rail
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Powered_Rail

To craft Powered Rail (makes 6), you need: Gold Ingot x6, Stick x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Gold Ingot x6, Stick x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Gold Ingot | (empty) | Gold Ingot
Gold Ingot | Stick | Gold Ingot
Gold Ingot | Redstone Dust | Gold Ingot
