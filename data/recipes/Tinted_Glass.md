# Tinted Glass
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Tinted_Glass

To craft Tinted Glass (makes 2), you need: Amethyst Shard x4, Glass x1.
Ingredients: Amethyst Shard x4, Glass x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Amethyst Shard | (empty)
Amethyst Shard | Glass | Amethyst Shard
(empty) | Amethyst Shard | (empty)
