# Weathered Copper Bulb
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Weathered_Copper_Bulb

To craft Weathered Copper Bulb (makes 4), you need: Weathered Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Weathered Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Weathered Copper | (empty)
Weathered Copper | Blaze Rod | Weathered Copper
(empty) | Redstone Dust | (empty)
