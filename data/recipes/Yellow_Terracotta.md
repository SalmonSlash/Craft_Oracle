# Yellow Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Yellow_Terracotta

To craft Yellow Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Yellow Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Yellow Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Yellow Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
