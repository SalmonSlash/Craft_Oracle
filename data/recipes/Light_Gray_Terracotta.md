# Light Gray Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Light_Gray_Terracotta

To craft Light Gray Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Light Gray Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Light Gray Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Light Gray Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
