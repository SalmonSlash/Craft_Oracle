# Spectral Arrow
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spectral_Arrow

To craft Spectral Arrow (makes 2), you need: Glowstone Dust x4, Arrow x1.
Ingredients: Glowstone Dust x4, Arrow x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Glowstone Dust | (empty)
Glowstone Dust | Arrow | Glowstone Dust
(empty) | Glowstone Dust | (empty)
