# Spire Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spire_Armor_Trim

To craft Spire Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Spire Armor Trim x1, Purpur Block x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Spire Armor Trim x1, Purpur Block x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Spire Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Purpur Block | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
