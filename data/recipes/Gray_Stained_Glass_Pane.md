# Gray Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Gray_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Gray Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Gray Stained Glass x5, Gray Dye or Gray Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Gray Stained Glass x5, Gray Dye or Gray Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Gray Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
