# Host Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Host_Armor_Trim

To craft Host Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Host Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Host Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Host Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Terracotta | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
