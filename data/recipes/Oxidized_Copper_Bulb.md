# Oxidized Copper Bulb
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Oxidized_Copper_Bulb

To craft Oxidized Copper Bulb (makes 4), you need: Oxidized Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Oxidized Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Oxidized Copper | (empty)
Oxidized Copper | Blaze Rod | Oxidized Copper
(empty) | Redstone Dust | (empty)
