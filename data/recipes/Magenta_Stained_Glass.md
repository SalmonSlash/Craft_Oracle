# Magenta Stained Glass
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Magenta_Stained_Glass

To craft Magenta Stained Glass (makes 8), you need: Glass x8, Magenta Dye x1.
Ingredients: Glass x8, Magenta Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass | Glass | Glass
Glass | Magenta Dye | Glass
Glass | Glass | Glass
