# Dune Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Dune_Armor_Trim

To craft Dune Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Dune Armor Trim x1, Sandstone x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Dune Armor Trim x1, Sandstone x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Dune Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Sandstone | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
