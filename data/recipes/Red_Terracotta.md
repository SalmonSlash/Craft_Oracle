# Red Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Red_Terracotta

To craft Red Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Red Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Red Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Red Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
