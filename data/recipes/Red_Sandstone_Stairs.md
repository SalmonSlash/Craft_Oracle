# Red Sandstone Stairs
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Red_Sandstone_Stairs

To craft Red Sandstone Stairs (makes 4), you need: Red Sandstone or Cut Red Sandstone or Chiseled Red Sandstone x6.
Ingredients: Red Sandstone or Cut Red Sandstone or Chiseled Red Sandstone x6
