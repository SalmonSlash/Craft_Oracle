# Green Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Green_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Green Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Green Stained Glass x5, Green Dye or Green Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Green Stained Glass x5, Green Dye or Green Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Green Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
