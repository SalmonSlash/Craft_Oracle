# Campfire
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Campfire

To craft Campfire, you need: Stick x3, Coal or Charcoal x1, Oak Log or Oak Wood or Birch Log x3.
Ingredients: Stick x3, Coal or Charcoal x1, Oak Log or Oak Wood or Birch Log x3

(empty) | Stick | (empty)
