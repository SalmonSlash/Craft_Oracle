# Bamboo Hanging Sign
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Bamboo_Hanging_Sign

To craft Bamboo Hanging Sign (makes 6), you need: Iron Chain x2, Block of Stripped Bamboo x6.
Ingredients: Iron Chain x2, Block of Stripped Bamboo x6
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Iron Chain | (empty) | Iron Chain
Block of Stripped Bamboo | Block of Stripped Bamboo | Block of Stripped Bamboo
Block of Stripped Bamboo | Block of Stripped Bamboo | Block of Stripped Bamboo
