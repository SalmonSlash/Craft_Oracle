# Brown Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Brown_Terracotta

To craft Brown Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Brown Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Brown Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Brown Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
