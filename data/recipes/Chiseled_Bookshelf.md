# Chiseled Bookshelf
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Chiseled_Bookshelf

To craft Chiseled Bookshelf, you need: Planks x6, Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x3.
Ingredients: Planks x6, Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Planks | Planks | Planks
Oak Slab | Oak Slab | Oak Slab
Planks | Planks | Planks
