# Lime Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Lime_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Lime Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Lime Stained Glass x5, Lime Dye or Lime Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Lime Stained Glass x5, Lime Dye or Lime Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Lime Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
