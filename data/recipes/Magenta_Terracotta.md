# Magenta Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Magenta_Terracotta

To craft Magenta Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Magenta Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Magenta Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Magenta Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
