# Rib Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Rib_Armor_Trim

To craft Rib Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Rib Armor Trim x1, Netherrack x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Rib Armor Trim x1, Netherrack x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Rib Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Netherrack | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
