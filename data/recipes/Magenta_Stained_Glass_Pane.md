# Magenta Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Magenta_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Magenta Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Magenta Stained Glass x5, Magenta Dye or Magenta Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Magenta Stained Glass x5, Magenta Dye or Magenta Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Magenta Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
