# Black Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Black_Terracotta

To craft Black Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Black Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Black Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Black Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
