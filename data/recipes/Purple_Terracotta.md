# Purple Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Purple_Terracotta

To craft Purple Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Purple Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Purple Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Purple Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
