# End Crystal
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/End_Crystal

To craft End Crystal, you need: Glass x7, Eye of Ender x1, Ghast Tear x1.
Ingredients: Glass x7, Eye of Ender x1, Ghast Tear x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass | Glass | Glass
Glass | Eye of Ender | Glass
Glass | Ghast Tear | Glass
