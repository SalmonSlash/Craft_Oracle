# Exposed Copper Bulb
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Exposed_Copper_Bulb

To craft Exposed Copper Bulb (makes 4), you need: Exposed Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Exposed Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Exposed Copper | (empty)
Exposed Copper | Blaze Rod | Exposed Copper
(empty) | Redstone Dust | (empty)
