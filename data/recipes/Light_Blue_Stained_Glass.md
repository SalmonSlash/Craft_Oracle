# Light Blue Stained Glass
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Light_Blue_Stained_Glass

To craft Light Blue Stained Glass (makes 8), you need: Glass x8, Light Blue Dye x1.
Ingredients: Glass x8, Light Blue Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass | Glass | Glass
Glass | Light Blue Dye | Glass
Glass | Glass | Glass
