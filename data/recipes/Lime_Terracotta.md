# Lime Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Lime_Terracotta

To craft Lime Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Lime Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Lime Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Lime Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
