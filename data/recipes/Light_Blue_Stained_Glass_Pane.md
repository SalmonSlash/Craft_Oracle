# Light Blue Stained Glass Pane
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Light_Blue_Stained_Glass_Pane

To craft Light Blue Stained Glass Pane (makes 16), you need: Glass Pane or Light Blue Stained Glass x5, Light Blue Dye or Light Blue Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3.
Ingredients: Glass Pane or Light Blue Stained Glass x5, Light Blue Dye or Light Blue Stained Glass x1, Glass Pane x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Light Blue Dye | Glass Pane
Glass Pane | Glass Pane | Glass Pane
