# Detector Rail
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Detector_Rail

To craft Detector Rail (makes 6), you need: Iron Ingot x6, Stone Pressure Plate x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Iron Ingot x6, Stone Pressure Plate x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Iron Ingot | (empty) | Iron Ingot
Iron Ingot | Stone Pressure Plate | Iron Ingot
Iron Ingot | Redstone Dust | Iron Ingot
