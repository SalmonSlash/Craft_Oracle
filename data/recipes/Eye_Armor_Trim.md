# Eye Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Eye_Armor_Trim

To craft Eye Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Eye Armor Trim x1, End Stone x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Eye Armor Trim x1, End Stone x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Eye Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | End Stone | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
