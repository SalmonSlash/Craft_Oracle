# Copper Bulb
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Copper_Bulb

To craft Copper Bulb (makes 4), you need: Block of Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1.
Ingredients: Block of Copper x3, Blaze Rod x1, Redstone Dust x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
(empty) | Block of Copper | (empty)
Block of Copper | Blaze Rod | Block of Copper
(empty) | Redstone Dust | (empty)
