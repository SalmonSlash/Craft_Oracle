# Netherite Upgrade
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Netherite_Upgrade

To craft Netherite Upgrade (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Netherite Upgrade x1, Netherrack x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Netherite Upgrade x1, Netherrack x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Netherite Upgrade | Diamond
Diamond | Netherrack | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
