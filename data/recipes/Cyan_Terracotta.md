# Cyan Terracotta
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cyan_Terracotta

To craft Cyan Terracotta (makes 8), you need: Terracotta x8, Cyan Dye x1.
Ingredients: Terracotta x8, Cyan Dye x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
Terracotta | Cyan Dye | Terracotta
Terracotta | Terracotta | Terracotta
