# Wild Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wild_Armor_Trim

To craft Wild Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Wild Armor Trim x1, Mossy Cobblestone x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Wild Armor Trim x1, Mossy Cobblestone x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Wild Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Mossy Cobblestone | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
