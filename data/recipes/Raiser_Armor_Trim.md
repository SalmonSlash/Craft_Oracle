# Raiser Armor Trim
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Raiser_Armor_Trim

To craft Raiser Armor Trim (makes 2), you need: Diamond x7, Raiser Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1.
Ingredients: Diamond x7, Raiser Armor Trim x1, Terracotta x1
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Diamond | Raiser Armor Trim | Diamond
Diamond | Terracotta | Diamond
Diamond | Diamond | Diamond
