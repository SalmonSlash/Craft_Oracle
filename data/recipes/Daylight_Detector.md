# Daylight Detector
source: https://minecraft.wiki/w/Daylight_Detector

To craft Daylight Detector, you need: Glass x3, Nether Quartz x3, Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x3.
Ingredients: Glass x3, Nether Quartz x3, Oak Slab or Birch Slab or Acacia Slab x3
Grid (rows of the crafting table):
Glass | Glass | Glass
Nether Quartz | Nether Quartz | Nether Quartz
Oak Slab | Oak Slab | Oak Slab
